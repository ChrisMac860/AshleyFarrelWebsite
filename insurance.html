Insurance

Make sure your family and property are protected

From property to life cover, we will search for the right insurance policy for you

Sometimes in life, the unexpected can occur and you need to make sure you're protected against this. At Farrell Financial Solutions, we will make it our goal to search through a wide selection of insurance policies to the find the right one for you. With Farrell Financial Solutions, you can leave the hard work to us.

We can assist you with the following insurance policies:

  • Life insurance

  • Critical illness cover insurance

  • Life insurance with critical illness

  • Buildings and contents insurance

  • Income protection insurance

  • Unemployment cover

  • Accident and sickness cover

We also believe in arranging your policies properly and writing them in trust. By doing this we ensure that any proceeds of a claim go the right hands at the right time avoiding financial embarrassment caused by probate delays and taxation. Trusts are not regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority.

Please contact us to make sure your policies are set up correctly.
